Styling Guide

Best Practices

  • Pewter and oxidised silver look especially luxurious on you

  • The depth of a muted olive or dusty teal is your signature strength

Styling Guide

Best Practices

  • Extremely bold, clear colours are your absolute best
  • High contrast black-and-white is a signature look for you
  • Both very light and very dark work — the key is clarity
  • Vivid colours look electric rather than overwhelming on you
  • White gold and platinum are your best metal choices

Styling Guide

Best Practices

  • High contrast is your superpower — black and white together
  • Bold clear colours look striking, never overwhelming

Styling Guide

Best Practices

  • Darkness is your natural energy — very deep colours ground you
  • Cool near-blacks and dark navies are your most powerful neutrals
  • White gold and oxidised silver are more interesting than bright silver
  • Avoid warm tones even in neutrals — they create disconnect
  • You look extraordinary in monochromatic deep tonal dressing
